## Safety notes

- NCCP 00689 applies carboplatin/etoposide count and organ-function rules only during induction; the maintenance phase uses atezolizumab immune-toxicity logic.
- NCCP 00310 and 00281 distinguish Day 1 cycle-start thresholds from Day 8 gemcitabine modification rules.
- These are encoded prototypes and do not replace the current official protocol, clinical judgement, local policy or pharmacy verification.
