Regimen-specific NCCP day-ward protocol decision support

Demonstration prototype only. This application does not prescribe, authorise or confirm the safety of treatment. It must not replace the current NCCP regimen, local policy or independent clinical judgement. Do not enter identifiable patient information.
iPhone use: open this application from a normal Safari web address or add it to the Home Screen. The Mail/Files preview may display the interface without running its decision engine.
