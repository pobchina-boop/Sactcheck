Commit title:
Version 0.32.0 · Batch 5 early breast expansion

Description:
Expands SACTCheck from 48 to 58 active NCCP regimens with ten non-duplicate early breast cancer protocols spanning neoadjuvant and adjuvant treatment. Adds TNBC carboplatin/paclitaxel to dose-dense AC schedules, distinct IV, subcutaneous and Phesgo HER2 regimens, dose-dense pembrolizumab variants, a general pertuzumab/trastuzumab chemotherapy protocol and adjuvant ribociclib. Restores the official NCCP PDF link for regimen 00857 and adds a catalogue-wide release check requiring every active protocol to include an official HSE NCCP document URL. Retains partial-assessment behaviour and the central ECOG placeholder. All additions remain encoded prototypes pending formal consultant and oncology-pharmacy validation.
