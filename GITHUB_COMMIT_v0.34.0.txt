Summary
Version 0.34.0 · Batch 6 early breast expansion

Description
Expands SACTCheck from 58 to 68 active NCCP regimens with ten non-duplicate neoadjuvant and adjuvant breast cancer protocols. Adds TCH, AC followed by weekly paclitaxel, two dose-dense AC-paclitaxel schedules, three HER2-directed AC-TH schedules, FEC100, adjuvant abemaciclib and the 21-day trastuzumab DDAC-TH schedule.

Retains blood-first rapid assessment, non-blocking partial and single-value entry, compact optional inputs, explicit patient-value versus protocol-cutoff comparisons, the TCHP correction, official NCCP PDF validation, duplicate safeguards and the pending emetogenic-risk/supportive-medications framework. Where thresholds differ by treatment phase, the new encodings return a conservative phase-review result rather than assuming an anthracycline or paclitaxel phase.

The active catalogue now contains 68 unique protocol IDs and paths. New protocol encodings remain prototypes pending formal consultant and oncology-pharmacy validation.
