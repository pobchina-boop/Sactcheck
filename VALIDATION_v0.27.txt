Safety regression checks encoded
--------------------------------
- Low ANC alone does not trigger an automatic protocol hold for single-agent durvalumab.
- Low platelets alone do not trigger an automatic protocol hold for single-agent atezolizumab.
- FBC abnormalities can be flagged for clinical review without inventing an NCCP numerical threshold.
- Pneumonitis, hepatitis, colitis, nephritis, endocrine toxicity, severe rash/SCAR,
  myocarditis, myositis, serious neurological toxicity and infusion reactions retain
  protocol-specific withhold or permanent-discontinuation pathways.
- Cytotoxic count rules remain available for chemotherapy-containing regimens.
- Selected indication is included in the generated assessment context and copyable summary.

Limitations and governance
--------------------------
- This remains an encoded demonstration prototype.
- Consultant and oncology-pharmacy validation are still required before clinical deployment.
- The update corrects identified systematic ICI template contamination but does not constitute
  a completed clinical audit of every protocol in the library.
- Missing optional values are not assumed normal; unassessed relevant domains remain visible.
