Clinical limitations
--------------------
Automated validation is not clinical validation. The encoded output must not replace the current NCCP PDF, prescribing review, clinician judgement, oncology-pharmacy verification, local policy or governance.

NCCP 00897 v3 remains listed in the current official NCCP gastrointestinal regimen library, but its printed review date was 20 February 2026. Recheck the source version during formal validation before clinical activation.
